For Those About To Slack, We Salute You!

Welcome to the ATDC Slack community. Like the rest of the ATDC, we're here to help people create and grow technology companies that drive impact.

We've started this Slack community as a way for our companies, coaches, and staff to communicate, network and solve problems together. This community is meant for ATDC Accelerate and Signature founders and employees, ATDC staff, and ATDC mentors.

By joining this network you're agreeing to a couple of simple rules:

  1. Be Nice
  2. Be Helpful
  3. Be Inclusive
  4. Make Great Things
Join Today!

# Our Channels

Open to Everyone

  • general: General information and going ons with the ATDC.
  • random: The water cooler (or Waffle House coffee station) talk of the Slack community.
  • office-hours: A reminder for the office hours schedule. Also, please feel free to offer suggestions on ways to make the office hours better or give a :+1: to a particularly good experience.
  • news: News of interest. We'll try to spotlight any ATDC company news in this channel. If we miss something or you find something particularly interesting, then please post.
  • events: Events at the ATDC and events y'all might find interesting around Atlanta.
  • atdc-marketplace: A channel to suggest new market place offerings and ask support-type questions on the current offerings.
  • fintech All the news fit to share about the important world of #fintech Moderated by Charles E. Potts
  • retailtech: A place to discuss and share links about RetailTech. Moderated by Monique Mills
  • idears: Ideas for the ATDC and, well, life.
  • topscore: High score for basketball and maybe other things... soon. Pics or didn't happen.
